lab two

The Lab 2 project was to design a receiver that could detect the presence of a low frequency light signal while filtering out other sources of light, and upon detecting the desired signal's absence, will send an email to notify the user of a break in the signal.


Key features include a breadboard with an Arduino UNO microcontroller board. The UNO determines if the signal is present based on the filter's output, then uses serial communication to trigger an email that will be sent via a Python script.




highlights:




- Arduino UNO microcontroller board connected to breadboard for receiving light signals
- digital filter code programmed to the UNO
- email alert communication through serial connection
